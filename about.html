Hey! Nice to meet you!

Hi, I'm Kyle Scollin, an enthusiastic designer based in the Bay Area.

I grew up on a farm in Northern California. Being surrounded by nature I had a lot of areas to play and exercise my imagination. Ironically, I ended up finding my passion playing with computers. In fact, it was a computer game (Myth II Soulblighter) that gave me my interest in graphic design.

In college I studied computer science for 2 years before deciding my passion was design. This background led me to a job at Google designing and developing mobile ads.

Fastforward to today, and I have spent the past few years in a start-up leading a team of six, tackling a wide variety of responsibilities. Along with growing, training and managing the team, I am also responsible for all of the branding of the company, helping design the products, supporting the sales team to pitch, sell, design, and build mobile ads, and yes, even occasionally choosing wall colors.

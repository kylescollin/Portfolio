Mobile Ads

I have been designing and building mobile ads for nearly 8 years. Typically these are ads for Fortune 500 companies as well as many others. I’ve designed ads with the client's assets and also pitched my own creative concepts making sure to adhere to the++ brand guidelines. For the past 4 years I have built, trained, and managed a team of 6 who have created some really innovative advertisements.

Amope

This was a pitch for Amope. The concept was to allow users to explore the various features of the product in a connected space. The first panel is meant to be simple and concise to quickly grab the user's attention and build product recognition. Users who were interested in the product could swipe around and explore all of the features.

Flipz

This was a pitch for Flipz. This simple idea was for users to shake their phone and have the Flipz pretzels come flying out of the bag.

Axe

This was a pitch for Axe. Everyone knows them for their body spray but the goal for the client was to feature their wide variety of products. The goal with this ad was to feature all of their products as a collection, hoping to entice users to invest in each product for its unique purpose.

Google

This was a pitch for Google. They were featuring their home wifi. The goal was to have a very clean, simple, animated experience allowing users to tap on areas to explore.

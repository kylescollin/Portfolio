Fox & Bear Co.

My fiancé and I decided to start our own side business making baked goods, pickles, and a variety of other treats. We worked together to brand ourselves and build up a business.

Logo

After sketching some logo ideas we found that we loved the imagery of a tree. It evokes the idea of nature, and wilderness, but also family and heritage which represents the brand we want to achieve. So I began by stipple drawing a tree and then creating the typographic treatment. We decided the typographic treatment was strong enough, so we opted to lead with that and use the tree as a supporting element.

Website

I designed and hand coded a 5 page website. Every photo that we used on the site was taken by me. We setup an ordering system where people could submit orders through the website and choose a pick up time. However we soon discovered we were more effective just taking pre-orders and delivering in big batches.

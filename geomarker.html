GeoMarker

PlaceIQ needed a tool to better construct their mapping platform. The goal of the tool is to show a user a specific building that needs to be mapped, highlight the address and then have the user draw a precise shape around the walls of the building. The tool was built rapidly leaving little time for optimizations.
